WHO WE ARE

Get ready to crank it up with The JettAways… a full-throttle tribute to the music, attitude, and rebellious spirit of Joan Jett & The Blackhearts.

Fueled by roaring guitars, pounding drums, and a no-apologies rock 'n' roll edge, The JettAways deliver the songs that defined generations of rock fans. From the fist-pumping anthem "I Love Rock 'n' Roll" to crowd favorites like "Bad Reputation," "Do You Want to Touch Me," "Cherry Bomb," "Crimson & Clover," and "I Hate Myself for Loving You," every show is a high-energy ride through one of rock's most iconic catalogs.

Fronted by powerhouse vocals that capture Joan's unmistakable grit and backed by a band of seasoned musicians who live for the stage, The JettAways don't just play the songs… they bring the attitude, the leather, the volume, and the raw energy that made Joan Jett a rock legend.
