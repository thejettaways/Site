ABOUT THE BAND

Four people who decided the world needed more snarl and a lot less ballad.

THE STORY

The JettAways came together because too many tribute bands play it safe. Too polished. Too clean. Too eager to please. Joan Jett — the first woman to launch her own record label, the woman who told the radio program directors of America to go to hell and put out "I Love Rock 'n Roll" anyway — deserves louder.

“Girls have got balls. They're just a little higher up, that's all.” — Joan Jett
“Rock and roll is supposed to be fun.” — Joan Jett

WHAT WE'RE NOT

NOT A NOSTALGIA ACT

NOT POLITE
